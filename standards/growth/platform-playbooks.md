# Platform Playbooks Reference

## Usage
This is a condensed reference for platform-specific growth tactics. Agents read this as background context when producing channel playbooks or go-to-market plans. For full deep-dives, see the channel-playbook skill.

---

## Twitter/X
- Engagement velocity in first hour determines reach
- Replies > likes for algorithm weight; native content (no links) gets boosted
- Thread format: hook tweet, 3-5 value tweets, CTA tweet (numbered)
- Ratio: 3 value : 1 promo : 1 engagement post
- Avoid: link-only tweets, thread dumps without hooks, corporate tone

## LinkedIn
- Comments weigh 5x likes; native content preferred over links
- Document/carousel posts get 3x reach vs text-only
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences), hook in first line (before "see more")
- Carousels: 8-12 slides, educational, branded, CTA on last slide
- Avoid: hashtag spam (max 3-5), direct sales pitches, cross-posted tweets

## Reddit
- 90-9-1 rule: value-first, always
- Entry protocol: lurk 2 weeks, start with comments, earn karma first
- Self-promotion ratio: 9 helpful contributions per 1 self-promotional post; disclose affiliation
- Find 3-5 subreddits, check activity and moderator stance on self-promo
- Avoid: vote manipulation, corporate speak, ignoring mod feedback, shill accounts

## TikTok
- Completion rate is the top algorithm signal; 3-second hook is critical
- Formula: Hook (0-3s), Context (3-8s), Value (8-45s), CTA (last 3s)
- 1-3x daily during growth phase; use trending sounds within 48 hours
- Mix broad and niche hashtags, 3-5 per post
- Raw/authentic beats polished; avoid repurposed Instagram content

## Instagram
- Reels for discovery, Stories for engagement, Feed for brand portfolio
- Reels: 15-30s, text overlay for sound-off, cover image for grid
- Carousels: up to 10 slides, educational/storytelling, save rates matter
- Stories: use polls/quizzes/questions to boost engagement signals
- Niche hashtags (10K-500K posts), 10-15 per post in caption

## Product Hunt
- Launch Tuesday-Thursday, post at 12:01 AM PST, prepare assets 1 week ahead
- Build a coming-soon page and collect followers pre-launch
- First hour: activate network for genuine engagement; comments > upvotes
- Reply to every comment within 1 hour; be personal, not corporate
- Assets: 6 screenshots, 1 demo video (60-90s), tagline under 60 chars

## Hacker News
- Show HN: title prefix required; post weekday mornings US time
- Technical depth valued; explain architecture, not just features
- What works: open source, novel tech, transparent posts, honest post-mortems
- Contribute thoughtful technical responses; never ask for upvotes
- Avoid: marketing language, growth hacking, astroturfing, voting rings
